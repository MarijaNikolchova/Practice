Before you explore, there are a few things you may be curious about!

What is this website?

This website is a place where everyone can read and share short stories, poems, and any other form of media within 100,000 characters! Create enjoyable media for all to enjoy, or read the media already created!

Who is the web designer?

My name is Marija. I graduated from the Faculty of Philology as an English and Literature Major. I am working to become a web developer aswell. This website is my first web design project! I am also following my passion for writing through this website.

Who can submit their work?

Anyone with an account here can submit their work! As long as you're willing to put your work out there, you can make an account and get started! If you just want to read, though, you don't need an account! You do, however, need one to rate the works of others. Ready to explore?
